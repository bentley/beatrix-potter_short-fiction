List of Illustrations

  1. Mrs. Rabbit (a rabbit wearing a blue dress and white apron) tucks Peter into bed. Only the tips of his ears and paws are visible.

  2. Rabbits peek out from underneath and around the roots of a tree.

  3. Mrs. Rabbit says goodbye to her children. Peter, in his blue coat, is looking away from Mrs. Rabbit.

  4. Mr. McGregor, knife and fork in hand, looks at a steaming pie being brought to him by Mrs. McGregor.

  5. Mrs. Rabbit buttons up Peter’s coat while his siblings walk away.

  6. Mrs. Rabbit walks through the wood with her basket and umbrella. She’s wearing a red scarf, red coat, and a yellow and black striped skirt.

  7. Peter’s siblings are picking blackberries, while blackbirds try to steal them.

  8. Peter runs over the fields, in his blue coat.

  9. Peter squeezes under a wooden gate, which is tugging on his coat.

  10. Peter sits in the garden with a carrot in each paw, and another on the ground next to him. Behind him, perched on a fork handle, a robin in singing.

  11. Peter walks through a vegetable garden, with his paws on his tummy.

  12. Mr. McGregor kneels on the ground with a tool for planting small plants. He looks surprised to see Peter.

  13. Peter, looking scared, runs across a green lawn, pursued by Mr. McGregor waving a rake.

  14. One of Peter’s shoes lies under a cabbage plant, watched by a yellow bird.

  15. Peter tumbles over, with a button on his coat stuck in a net under a gooseberry bush.

  16. Three sparrows tweet at Peter, who is still stuck in the net and looks sad.

  17. Peter and the three sparrows flee from underneath the sieve, leaving his blue jacket on the ground.

  18. Peter jumps into a watering can as water splashes out of the top.

  19. Peter’s ears stick out from the watering can, while Mr. McGregor hunts under flowerpots in the background.

  20. Peter escapes from the potting shed, followed by Mr. McGregor’s boot. He knocks over three geraniums in pots as he jumps.

  21. Peter stands, dripping wet, in a flowerbed, next to a robin.

  22. Peter runs up to a closed door in a garden wall.

  23. Peter tries to squeeze through the door, while a mouse watches him.

  24. A white cat looks into a pond filled with goldfish, who look back. Peter is in the distance on the other side of a lawn.

  25. Peter sits in a wheelbarrow, looking out over a field with a gate on the other side. Mr. McGregor is directly between Peter and the gate.

  26. Peter runs down a path and under a gate. Mr. McGregor is in the distance, chasing Peter with a rake.

  27. Peter’s blue coat and little black shoes are hanging from a stick in a vegetable garden. Three blackbirds look on, while Mr. McGregor tends to the cabbages in the background.

  28. Peter arrives at the entrance to the family burrow. His three siblings, still in their red coats, look on.

  29. Peter sleeps on his side, while his mother in her blue dress and white apron looks at him while cooking. His three siblings look on from the entrance to the burrow.

  30. Mother heats some tea in a kettle over the fire, while Peter watches from his bed.

  31. Flopsy, Mopsy and Cotton-tail hold spoons and stand around a tall blue pot and pitcher. There’s a basket of blackberries on the floor.
